# Community conduct

Praxa contributors are expected to communicate with care, focus criticism on
the work, respect boundaries, and make technical discussions accessible to
people with different communication styles and backgrounds.

Harassment, threats, discrimination, doxxing, sexualized attention, deliberate
misrepresentation, and publication of private information are not acceptable.
Maintainers may edit, reject, lock, or remove contributions and participation
that violate these expectations.

Report conduct concerns privately to the Praxa Labs maintainers through the
repository's private reporting channel. Reports will be reviewed as promptly
and confidentially as practical.
